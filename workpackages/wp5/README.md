### WP5 – Fracture and fatigue in endovascular stents

**Leader**: Israel García (Universidad de Sevilla), Adrià Quintanas (BSC)

**Participants**: US, BSC

**Goal**: Simulate fracture in shape-memory alloy stents under service conditions (mainly fatigue), enabling patient-specific modeling strategies.

**Tasks include**:
- Review of in-vitro and in-vivo stent failures
- Simulation of literature experiments using the developed formulation
- Full-scale in-vivo simulations integrating Alya's fluid-structure interaction
- Validation and clinical input from Juan R. Cebral (GMU)
